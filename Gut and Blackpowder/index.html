THE INFECTED HORDE

The Zapper (slow heavy)

The standard, slow-moving infected. but they are dangerous in large groups. Focus headshots for effective crowd management.

Cuirassier (Heavy)

This heavily armored infected is slow but can perform a devastating charge. If you are hit by the charge, it will knock you back and momentarily immobilize you, requiring aid from an ally.

The Runner (Swift)

Extremely fast and aggressive. Requires rapid and precise suppression fire at a distance. Missing a close-range melee attack against a Runner is highly dangerous.

Igniter

The Igniter is a heavily burnt, shambling zombie with a distinctive yellow glow from its lantern. When an Igniter is within 100 studs of a player, it will throw its lantern, which explodes on impact to create a burning area that damages and debuffs players.

Bomber

The Bomber is a destructive zombie that carries a barrel and a torch, stopping near a target before lighting its fuse.

MILITARY SPECIALIZATIONS

Line Infantry

The backbone of the defense. Focuses on steady, disciplined fire, bayonet defense,pikes, and holding formation against overwhelming odds.

  • **Primary Weapon:** Smoothbore Musket,Sabre,Pike
  • **Key Skill:** has a high amount of weapons to choose from
  • **Role:** Sustained damage and crowd control.

Field Surgeon

Crucial for morale and survival. Equipped to rapidly treat wounds. Their presence keeps the fight going.

  • **Primary Weapon:** Sabre
  • **Key Skill:** Field Dressing (Rapidly heal a wounded comrade)
  • **Role:** Essential support and medical aid.

Seamen

Specialist in heavy arms and explosives. Essential for breaking up large enemy formations and destroying heavily armored targets like the Cuirassier.

  • **Primary Weapon:** Sabre and Blunderbuss
  • **Key Skill:** being able to use a large variety Gun for large crowd control
  • **Role:** Anti-armor and crowd control.

The Officer

The leader on the battlefield. Officers provide crucial tactical guidance and command, boosting the effectiveness and resilience of nearby troops.

  • **Primary Weapon:** Sabre and Pistol
  • **Key Skill:** Inspire Troops (Buffs damage allies with a charge that boost the speed and damge of allys around him)
  • **Role:** Leadership.

The Sapper

The fortifications expert. The Sapper specializes in building defenses like barricades and Spikes to control the flow of the infected, and to protect allys from them aswell.

  • **Primary Tool/Weapon:** Hammer/Claw Hammer.Large Axe
  • **Key Skill:** Fortification (Constructs and repairs defenses)
  • **Role:** Defense construction and obstacle creation.
